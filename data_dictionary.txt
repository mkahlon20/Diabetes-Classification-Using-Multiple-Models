Diabetes Dataset

Pregnancies - Integer - Number of pregnancies
Glucose - Integer - Blood glucose level
BloodPressure - Integer - Blood pressure measurement
BMI - Float - Body mass index
Outcome - Integer - Diabetes diagnosis (0 = no diabetes, 1 = diabetes)


Heart Disease Dataset

age - Integer - Age of the patient

sex - Integer - Gender of the patient
0 = female
1 = male

cp - Integer - Chest pain type

trestbps - Integer - Resting blood pressure

chol - Integer - Serum cholesterol level

fbs - Integer - Fasting blood sugar
1 = fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl
0 = otherwise

restecg - Integer - Resting electrocardiographic results

thalach - Integer - Maximum heart rate achieved

exang - Integer - Exercise induced angina
1 = yes
0 = no

oldpeak - Float - ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest

slope - Integer - Slope of the peak exercise ST segment

ca - Integer - Number of major vessels colored by fluoroscopy

thal - Integer - Thalassemia defect type

target - Integer - Presence of heart disease
0 = no heart disease
1 = heart disease
